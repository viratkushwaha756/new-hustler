What services does HustlersConnect offer?

We offer three core services:

  • Website Design & Development: Custom websites, e-commerce solutions, and responsive design
  • Social Media Management: Content strategy, community management, and paid advertising
  • Video Editing & Production: Professional editing, motion graphics, and social media videos

We also provide consulting and strategy sessions to help you determine which services are right for your business.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

  • Basic websites: 2-4 weeks
  • E-commerce websites: 4-8 weeks
  • Social media setup: 1-2 weeks
  • Video editing projects: 48 hours to 1 week
  • Ongoing social media management: Ongoing with monthly reporting

We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on project scope and requirements:

  • Website projects: Start at $1,500 for basic sites, up to $10,000+ for complex e-commerce
  • Social media management: Monthly packages from $800 to $3,000+
  • Video editing: Per-project pricing from $299 to $1,999+
  • One-time consultations: $150 per hour

We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs. We also offer custom packages for businesses requiring multiple services.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer several support options:

  • Website maintenance plans: Starting at $150/month for updates, security, and backups
  • Social media management: Ongoing monthly packages
  • Video editing retainer: Priority service for regular content creators
  • Hourly support: Available for ad-hoc requests

All our projects include 30 days of post-launch support to ensure everything is working perfectly.

What is your process for starting a new project?

Our 4-step process ensures smooth project execution:

  • Step 1: Discovery Call - We discuss your goals, timeline, and budget
  • Step 2: Proposal & Agreement - We provide a detailed proposal and contract
  • Step 3: Project Execution - We work on your project with regular updates
  • Step 4: Launch & Support - We launch your project and provide ongoing support

We maintain open communication throughout the process and involve you in key decisions.

What technical requirements do I need to provide?

Requirements vary by service:

  • For websites: Domain name, hosting details (or we can provide), brand assets, content
  • For social media: Login access to your accounts, brand guidelines, content ideas
  • For video editing: Raw footage, music preferences, brand colors, reference videos

We provide a detailed checklist for each service after project confirmation to make the process smooth for you.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options:

  • For larger projects: 50% deposit, 25% mid-point, 25% upon completion
  • For monthly services: Payment at the beginning of each month
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer, PayPal, credit cards
  • Custom plans: Available for established businesses with regular needs

We're flexible and can work with you to create a payment schedule that fits your budget.

How do we communicate during the project?

We use multiple communication channels to ensure you're always informed:

  • Weekly updates: Email summaries of progress
  • Project management tools: Trello or Asana for task tracking
  • Video calls: Zoom or Google Meet for important discussions
  • Phone & WhatsApp: For quick questions and urgent matters
  • Shared drives: Google Drive or Dropbox for file sharing

You'll have a dedicated point of contact who will be your main liaison throughout the project.

Quick Questions

Brief answers to common quick questions

What are your working hours?

We work Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST. Emergency support is available outside these hours for ongoing clients.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We're experienced in managing time zone differences and international payment methods.

Do you sign NDAs?

Absolutely. We're happy to sign NDAs to protect your intellectual property and confidential business information.

What if I need revisions?

We include specific revision rounds in each package. Additional revisions are available at reasonable hourly rates.

Who owns the final work?

You own all final deliverables. We retain the right to showcase the work in our portfolio unless otherwise specified.

How do I get started?

Contact us through our website, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs.
